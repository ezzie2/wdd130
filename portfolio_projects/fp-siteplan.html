Site Purpose

As a parent, what is your biggest worry? Your kids!!!!!!! If your kids have Autism, ADHD, Depression or Anxiety you may find yourself not knowing what to do or where to turn for help. These are the most common mental disorders kids face today. Although not new, many still struggle to understand the best way to help those who have it. Our website will not only guide you to where you can get help, but also provide you with more resources. These resources are researched and proven to help students deal with their challenge. We will provide you with guides to practice at home and that can help students at school. We will provide you with ways to help them see their challenge as a strength and not a weakness. In addition, we provide you a way for you to track the work done by anyone who is helping your son/daughter.

Target Audience

This is geared towards anyone who has a child with mental illnesses. It could be a parent, legal guardian, social workers, foster parents, schools etc.
