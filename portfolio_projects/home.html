Our Mission

Our organization provides support to everyone who faces the task of raising and teaching children to young adults with mental disorders. Our team of volunteer therapists have designed a program that allows parents, legal guardians or schools to provide the necessary help to kids in order to grow up to be successful members of society despite their challenges. Our goal is to provide resources in the local level to connect those within the same city or state boundaries with one another. This creates a network of assistance. You are not alone and shouldn't feel like you are alone. You have a community behind you ready to help you at any time.
