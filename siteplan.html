Site Name

Extreme Rafting Adventures - the Ultimate Rafitng Experiece

Purpose and Goals

Our goal is to inform our customers of our services and convince them that the services that we provide are the best. We want to provide everyone with an experience they will never forget. We are located in three major states: Idaho, Montana, Utah and Wyoming. Our season begins late spring and ends early fall. If this is your first time, our customer service will connect you with certified guides who can guide you into choosing the best experience package for you. Even if it is not your first time, we still connect you with a certified guide to help you book the experience of a lifetime.

Target Audience

We provide rafting tours for all skills level. We offer tours for in shape individuals, outdoorsy types, families, work groups, groups, buddies, etc. Our early season begins late spring until late fall. During our early season, we provide high-quality wet suits. The weather is cooler, but group sizes are smaller and there are lots of animals to see. If you are an adrenaline seeker, the snowmelt from the mountains create the best rapids one can imagine. The summer provides the perfect temperatures for you to not be afraid to take a dip. One can also enjoy scenic floats and enjoy watching local wildlife. Fall will not dissapoint you. Witness mother nature's change of seasons surrounded by golden foliage.
Things to consider before booking with us is that you must be at least 12 years old. A parent or legal guardian must accompany anyone under the age of 17. Liability forms must be signed ahead of time. Once booked, you will receive an itenary with information such as guide's name, where to meet, transportation, rentals(wet suits), group size, etc.
